Travel to ....KESWICK

Enjoy the quiet life, surrounded by beautiful mountains and gorgeous lakes!

The town!

Keswick is most certainly the heart of the northen Lake District, and is surrounded by beautiful mountains such as Skiddaw. This enchanting town is sure to bewitch any visitor, whether you're returning for your anual holiday, or vistiting for the first time, perhaps from elsewhere in the world!

The Square!

No town would be complete without a beautiful town square, and keswick isn't any different. Of course a bonus that comes with Keswick, is the market that arrives every Saturday selling all sorts of interesting things!

Shops!

And finally, to sum up this brief introduction to Keswick, I'd like to mention two of my favourite shops in the town. Firstly, Booths, the supermarket that sell just about all the food you could want. The Christmas pudding there is amazing, and they even sell pumpkins at halloween! However, my favourite shop of all time has to be Ye Olde Friars. It's an excellent chocolate shop, and well worth a visit!

Top Five Nearby Walks!

  1. Skiddaw (Mountain)
  2. Catbells (Mountain)
  3. Latrigg (Mountain)
  4. Blencathra (Mountain)
  5. Derwentwater (Lake)
